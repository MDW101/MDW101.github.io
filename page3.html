Basis of Sonic 3

Sonic The Hedgehog 3 was released December 20, 2024. It is the third installment in the franchise.

This introduced Shadow The Hedgehog into the series, as he started out as a vengeful villain.

Shadow and crew faced of with Sonic & The Gang throughout the movie in a race of time to either succeed in or stop the destruction of Earth. At the end, Shadow changed his ways and sacrificed himself for everyone.
