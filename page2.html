Basis of Sonic 2

Sonic The Hedgehog 2 released April 8, 2022. A couple years after the 1st installment.

This movie introduced fan favorites Tails and Kunckles, with Knuckles siding witht the evil Dr. Eggman while Tails sided with his soon to be best friend, Sonic.

This started a race for a magical emerald called the Master Emerald, which Eggman planned to use for evil. Able to befriend Knuckles, Sonic & The Gang raced to rescue the world.
